About the Band

Born out of the underground music circuits of Kolkata, The Solstice is a progressive rock outfit carving out a visceral, defiant space in the independent scene. Melding structural complexity with raw, emotional vulnerability, our sound shifts between intricately layered guitar arrangements, sweeping neoclassical violin textures, and crushing rhythms that push past standard rock boundaries.

At our core, we write progressive rock ballads driven by themes of resilience, survival, and structural depth. From quiet, atmospheric builds to unyielding walls of sonic gravity, our music is a testament to those standing firm against the current—raw, uncompromised, and defiant.
